AI Improve — Relationships Policy

The AI Improve workflow intentionally excludes the relationships field from every stage of the process:

  1. Not sent to the model. Before calling DeepSeek, the node's relationships array is stripped (stripRelationships()), so the model never sees your existing links and cannot alter them.
  2. Not shown in the diff. Both the Original and Improved panels display the relationship-free payload, keeping the comparison focused on content fields only.
  3. Not updated on apply. When you click Apply Changes, your existing relationships are preserved and re-attached after the improved data is written back to the node.
Managing relationships? Use the AI Discover Relations feature (the amber Find Relations button) to discover and curate links to other nodes in the graph.
