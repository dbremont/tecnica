Epistemic practice is not formless. Every act of knowing—from a scientist calibrating an instrument to a neural network updating its weights—follows a structured process. It is bound by constraints, mediated by tools, and anchored in infrastructure.

This system captures the topology of that practice. It decomposes the continuum of inference into a discrete, hypergraph-structured taxonomy of 14 foundational categories.

By rendering these structures explicit, we transform the opaque history of justification into a navigable, queryable space. The map is not the territory, but it reveals the passes through the mountains.
